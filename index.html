GROUP LIFE

We believe that life is better when you are connected with others, which is why we encourage everyone to get involved in GroupLife here at ONE*! We offer 3 types of small groups:
Life Groups- long term groups that meet for up to 2 years together
Life Apps- short term groups that meet for 6-10 weeks at a time
Care Groups- specialized groups for people who are experiencing challenging life circumstances

What's Going On

Blended & Blessed™ is a FREE one-day live event and livestream for stepfamily couples, single parents, dating couples with kids, and those who care about blended families. This event will unpack five keys that are crucial to healthy stepfamily marriages. With some of today’s most trusted and respected experts, Blended & Blessed will challenge, inspire, and encourage you. Sign up today by Clicking Here.

REACH student ministry is finishing up a series called My Story, which is about the undeniable experiences of real people meeting a real God in the midst of this thing we call life. We all face trials and challenges, and we all need to learn to overcome them, and hearing the experiences of others can help us regain focus, gather courage and start to believe again. REACH is Tuesday nights at 7pm for students 6th-12th grade.

Our annual Easter Egg Hunt is coming up on April 15th! You won’t want to miss being a part of this exciting event! We will have over 65,000 eggs, jump castles, a petting zoo, face painting, pictures with the Easter bunny and more! You can sign up to be a part of this outreach event at by Clicking Here.
