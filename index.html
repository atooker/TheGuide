What’s Going On

ONE*s very first Vacation Bible School was incredible thanks to all the people who joined in to help! We had 282 registered kids, 115 salvations, 1092 pairs of shoes collected that we donated to Soles for Souls, and we gave over 130 Bibles away during the week! It was an amazing week for the kids who got to have fun and learn about God at the same time. If you want to join the ONE* Kids team and help kids find and follow Jesus Christ, click here to sign up! And make sure to check out our ONE* Kids Facebook Page for updates, pictures and more!
